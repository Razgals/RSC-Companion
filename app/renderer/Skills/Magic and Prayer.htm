Magic & Prayer

2 Secondary Combat Skills that have just as equal importance out of combat. Mages and Monks are always increasing in the Runescape Population. Many high level warriors in Runescape choose these 2 to get an advantage over other players. This guild will teach you how to use these 2 effectively and what your options are between the 2.

Runescape has it's own way of telling you if you have enough runes or not. When you put your mouse over the Spell Book in the top right icons, click Spells and put your mouse over the desired spell. It'll have a picture of the rune and a number near it. If the number is in Green, your set to go but if the number is in Red, you'll need more of that rune before being able to cast the spell.

The Wizards Guild is one of the best places for Wizards who are members. The Wizards Guild is the only place where you can buy the Soul Rune. The Magic Guild is located in Yanille, south of Ardougne and requires that you have level 66 magic before you can enter the guild. On the second floor, there is a rune store where you can buy the Soul Rune for 2000 gp. On the third floor there are 4 portals that teleport you to the other wizard towers in Runescape. In the basement are some zombies behind a caged wall so you can practice yours spells without being attacked
Prayer

Prayer is less complicated then Magic but doesn't have many alternatives. Prayer can only be raised by burying bones and most of the prayer spells are self enhancing, other then the popular Paralyze Monster prayer. Unlike any other skill, Prayer needs to be recharged after being used. Your charge depends on your level. The higher your Prayer level, the more Prayer Points you have. When your down to 0, you'll need to find a church and recharge your prayer at an Altar.

Like stated above, the only way you can increase your prayer level is by bones. You get bones by fighting and killing a certain monster. Here's is an experience table for the certain kinds of bones and how much experience you get from them.
