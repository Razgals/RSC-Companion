Cooking

Cooking is an important and profitable skill. You can cook a range of things from seafood to pies to simple meat.

Simple Cooking

The easiest food to cook is chicken meat. You can take their meat and cook it directly on a fire or on a range.

1. Go north of Lumbridge to the farms. There are lots of chickens running around there.

2. You will have to point at a chicken and click it. This way you will attempt to attack the chicken.

3. After killing the chicken, you will be left standing on meat and a pile of bones. Click on the meat and bones to take them.

4. Then, open your inventory and click on the bones to bury them. This gives you prayer experience. If you do not want prayer experience, drop the bones and don't pick them up again.

5. Now you will have meat that is ready to be cooked. Cooking meat is easy. You can either use a fire to cook it, or you can use a range. You can find a fire nearby or you can make one yourself. To make one, you will need a Woodcutters Axe (The one you start with) and Tinderbox. Cut down a tree, drop the logs, click the Tinderbox in your inventory and then click the logs on the ground. This will either start a fire or tell you "You have failed to light the logs". Keep doing this until the fire is made.

6. To cook the meat, you will have to go to your inventory and click the meat. Then, click on the fire to start cooking. You may either get your meat nicely cooked or it may accidentally get burnt.

To eat the meat when you are low on health, you will have to go to your inventory and click on your meat.
Uncookable Foods
There are some food items you don't need to cook to eat. You can eat cabbage, bananas, chocolate bars, cheese, tomatoes, and kebabs without the need to cook yourself.
Stew

To cook stew, you will need to have at least a Cooking level of 25.

1. You will have to obtain a bowl. Then, you will need to fill the bowl will water at a sink or fountain. Bowl's can be made using crafting (see crafting guide for more info) or obtained in the Cooking Guild.

2. Get a potato, which is available in Lumbridge farms, and a piece of cooked meat.

3. Use the potato and cooked meat with the bowl full of water to mix everything together. A fountain or sink will do the trick. Finally, cook the bowl on a stove.

Stew heals a total of 9 hit points. Useful for win your hits level is less then 30.
Curry

Curry is simply spiced stew. Curry requires level 60 cooking to make and you must be a member. After you have successfully made a bowl of stew (see above), use the Spice with the Bowl of Stew.

Spice is available at a market stall in Ardougne.
Wine

Wine is very easy and straightforward to mix. All you need is a jug full of water, grapes, and level 35 cooking. The jug and grapes can be found in the Cook's Guild southwest of Varrock city.

To mix wine, use the grapes with the water-filled jug. There is a chance that your wine can go bad when you mix the grapes.

Wine heals health, but also has a negative affect on your attack level. So only use Wine as a last resort or just for the sake of gaining Cooking experience.
Pie

Summery of making pie...

1. Get pot, jug of water, pie dish, and filling (red-berries, meat, apple)
2. Turn wheat into flour at a windmill.
3. Use pot of flour with jug of water to get dough.
4. Use dough with pie dish to get pie shell.
5. Use filling with pie shell to get uncooked pie.
6. Use uncooked pie with stove to cook it.

Pies are a little more advanced than simple cooking and require more ingredients to cook. You will have to be at least level 10 cooking to make pies.

1. You will have to get a pot, a jug, and a pie dish.

2. You will have to find a fountain or sink to fill your jug. Open your inventory, and then click on your jug. Then, use the jug with the fountain or sink.

3. Next, you will have to get flour. To get flour you will have to pick wheat from a field. The next step is to go inside a windmill to turn wheat into flour. The windmill is made up of three stories. You will have to climb up to the top floor using the ladders. Then, when you get to the top, you need to put your wheat in the hopper. Next, you will have to click on the hopper again to operate it. Then the grain will slide down to the bottom level while turning into flour. When you reach the bottom floor, click your pot and then click the flour. Now, you will have a pot full of flour.

4. To make dough you will have to click the water you have in your inventory and then click the flour, which is also in your inventory. This way, you are mixing them together to make dough. Now, you have a choice of making bread dough, pastry dough or pizza base. First try making bread dough. You can cook bread dough directly on a range. If you wanted to make pies, you would have selected pastry dough when mixing water with flour.

5. Then, you will have to put your pastry dough in a pie dish. This way, you will have a pie shell. You can now pick red berries and use them with your Pie shell.

6. The last step now is to cook the pie on a range.

Pizza

To cook a simple pizza, you need to have at least level 35 cooking.

1. The required ingredients are cheese, tomato, pot of flour, and jug of water. Cheese and tomatoes can be bought at the Port Sarim food store or found in Draynor Village.

2. Cooking a simple pizza requires you to first mix together the pot of flour and the water to make a pizza base.

3. Next, you need to place the tomato and cheese into the pizza base. Doing so, gets you an uncooked pizza. You can then cook it on a stove.

After you are done, you can add toppings such as meat and anchovies.

Cake

Baking a cake requires you to first gather a few supplies most of which are available in the Chef's Guild west of Varrock.

1. You will need a cake tin, milk, flour, and an egg. Cake Tin can be found in the Chef's Guild. To acquire milk, you will need to use an empty bucket with a cow. Flour requires you to pick wheat from a farm and then grind it in a Wind Mill. Next use a pot to hold the flour in. An egg can be found in any chicken farm such as the one north of Lumbridge.

2. Use the pot of flour with the cake tin to mix everything together. Doing so will get you an uncooked cake.

3. Next, just cook this cake on any oven.

After you have the cooked cake, you can add chocolate to it. Chocolate bars are available for free inside the Chef's Guild.

Chef's Guild

After your have proven yourself to be a good chef, you can enter the Chef's Guild just west of Varrock. You will need a Chef's Hat and a minimum of level 32 cooking to enter.

Inside the Chef's Guild, you will find various items to assist you in your cooking such as the following.

- Windmill
- Sinks
- Cooking Ranges
- Pie Dishes
- Pots
- Bowls
- Cake Tin
- Jugs
- Chocolate Bars
- Cooking Apples
- Grapes
